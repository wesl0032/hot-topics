Economics

The Value Of Money

Money is a system we all agreed on that determines value. It’s not “real” physically, but it affects a lot of real-life decisions, where they live, what opportunities they get, and what can be accessed. Money makes things efficient because there’s no barter system anymore, but there’s inequality depending on how it’s distributed and made. I don’t think money is good or bad on its own, it just depends on who has it and how it is used. In a way, it shapes people’s freedom which provides some sense of power but also messed up.

Health & Society

GMO Food

Genetically modified food can be helpful, especially for feeding large populations, making crops more durable or stronger, and dealing with climate issues. But I understand why people are skeptical, especially for long-term health effects, big companies controlling how food is distributed, and environmental impact. I don’t think GMO food is automatically bad, but I do think it needs to be properly regulated with transparency so people are aware of what they’re consuming and trust the system.

Future Technology

The View of the World in 2100

Technology and climate change will evolve making the world have to adapt. Automation and AI will be normalized in everyday life, and a lot of jobs will implement AI, potentially causing jobs to get cut. Climate issues will affect how and where people live, how cities are built, and an increased improvement for sustainability. I think the gap between rich and poor will get worse or forced to change based on world events ultimately improving the system. The future could advance rapidly, but it depends on if these resources are used responsibly and use technology.
